Farmer Finance Copilot

AI-powered financial safety tool that helps farmers in Uzbekistan understand profit, risks and safe loan sizes – and helps banks reduce non-performing loans.

Start Planning

Why it matters

  • Farmers often borrow without seeing real profit and risk.
  • Unexpected price or yield drops can lead to debt traps.
  • Banks struggle with manual risk analysis and NPLs.

The Problem

Farmers usually calculate their economics roughly or do not calculate at all. They do not see full production costs, realistic profit margins, or what happens if prices or yields decline. At the same time, banks need better tools to understand borrower risk and avoid bad loans.

There is no simple, visual, and data-driven tool that connects both sides: farmers and banks.

Key Pain Points

  • Hidden costs and over-optimistic income expectations.
  • No simulation of “bad season” scenarios.
  • Borrowing more than a farm can realistically repay.
